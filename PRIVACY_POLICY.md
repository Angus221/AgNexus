# Privacy Policy for AG Nexus

**Last Updated: December 10, 2024**

AG Nexus ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a browser extension designed to enhance productivity. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information We Collect

AG Nexus collects and stores the following information **locally on your device**:

### User-Provided Information
- **Nickname and Gender**: Voluntarily provided during initial setup for personalized interactions
- **API Keys**: Your AI service credentials (e.g., Alibaba DashScope API key)
- **Vault Data**: Passwords and sensitive information you choose to store

### Usage Data
- **Navigation Links**: Quick access URLs you save
- **Commands and Prompts**: Custom commands and AI prompts you create
- **Todo Items**: Task reminders and schedules
- **Chat History**: Conversations with the AI assistant (limited to 20 messages)
- **Memory Bank**: AI-generated summary of conversation context

## How We Store Your Data

**All data is stored locally** on your device using the browser's `chrome.storage.local` API. We do **NOT**:
- Upload your data to our servers
- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your personal information
- Track your browsing activity

## Data Transmission

When you use the AI assistant feature, your chat messages are sent to the **AI service provider you configure** (default: Alibaba DashScope). This transmission is:
- Initiated only when you send a message
- Sent directly to the API endpoint you specify
- Subject to the privacy policy of the AI service provider you choose

**We do not operate any servers that receive your data.**

## Data Security

- Vault data is protected by a PIN code you set
- API keys are stored locally and never transmitted to us
- All data remains on your device unless you explicitly export it

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- **Access**: View all stored data through the extension interface
- **Export**: Download your data using the export feature
- **Delete**: Clear chat history or remove any stored items
- **Uninstall**: Removing the extension deletes all locally stored data

## Children's Privacy

AG Nexus is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
